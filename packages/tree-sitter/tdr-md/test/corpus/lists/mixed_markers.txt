================
Mixed markers produce separate lists
================

- bullet one
- bullet two
1. ordered one
2. ordered two

---

(document
  (section
    (list
      (list_item
        (list_marker_minus)
        (paragraph
          (inline)))
      (list_item
        (list_marker_minus)
        (paragraph
          (inline))))
    (list
      (list_item
        (list_marker_dot)
        (paragraph
          (inline)))
      (list_item
        (list_marker_dot)
        (paragraph
          (inline))))))
